Our History

At Business Insight, we're passionate about empowering businesses to achieve their full potential through expert financial guidance and support. Founded in 2013 by a team of experienced financial professionals, we recognized the unique challenges that startups, MSMEs, and enterprises face in managing their finances effectively. We set out to create a company that would provide tailored, high quality financial services to help these businesses grow and thrive.

Over the years, we've built a reputation for delivering results and building long-lasting relationships with our clients. Our team has grown to include seasoned CFOs, financial analysts, and compliance experts who bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to every engagement. Today, we're proud to be a trusted partner to businesses across various industries, helping them navigate complex financial landscapes and achieve their growth objectives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower businesses of all sizes to reach their full potential by providing expert financial guidance, strategic insights, and personalized support. We strive to be a trusted partner to our clients, delivering innovative solutions that drive sustainable growth and long-term success.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every business, regardless of size or industry, has access to the high quality financial services they need to thrive. We aim to be the leading provider of CFO-as-a-service, recognized for our expertise, innovation, and unwavering commitment to our clients' success.
